Happy Birthday Cherth !

This poem is for my friend,
To the person I love the most,
And loves the most.

My relation with you,
Has been through its ups and downs,
But like the cosmos,
Where darkness represents my life,
And the stars as the clusters of my friendships,
You, with your beauty and kindess,
Has lit up my life,
Like that one special star,
That brightens up the whole night sky.

If I leave the world early,
And it makes you worry,
Remember that your presence was my happiness,
When my mind was stuck in the wilderness.

My love for you,
Will last for eternity,
Thus this poem,
Which will attain immortality,
That even if the world comes to an end,
You will always be,
MY BEST FRIEND.
